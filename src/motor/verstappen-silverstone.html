GREAT BRITAIN GP

Verstappen beats Norris and Hamilton in Silverstone

Henry Claudeau | 00:15 GMT-5
Share: |

Max Verstappen of Red Bull Racing soared to victory at the Great Britain Grand Prix, marking his impressive seventh triumph of the Formula 1 season. In a thrilling showcase of driving prowess at Silverstone, Verstappen clinched the top spot, outpacing his closest competitors, Lando Norris and Lewis Hamilton.

The race unfolded with Verstappen exhibiting exceptional skill and determination, maintaining a commanding lead throughout the whole Grand Prix. Verstappen's remarkable performance on the iconic english Silverstone circuit reaffirmed his status as a formidable force in Formula 1, securing a critical win that extends his lead in the championship standings.

Following Verstappen's dominant display, McLaren's Lando Norris finished a commendable second, trailing the Red Bull driver by a respectable 3.798 seconds. Mercedes' Lewis Hamilton crossed the finish line in third place, trailing Verstappen by 6.783 seconds.

The thrilling contest also witnessed strong performances from Oscar Piastri of McLaren, securing a fourth-place finish, and George Russell of Mercedes, claiming the fifth spot in the challenging race at Silverstone.

Verstappen's triumph at the Great Britain Grand Prix not only adds another accolade to his season but also consolidates his position as a frontrunner in the intense battle for the Formula 1 championship. His remarkable victory at Silverstone is a testament to his exceptional driving abilities and sets the stage for further excitement and competition in the upcoming races of the Formula 1 season.
